## Change
Increased ChunkedVec default chunk size from 8 to 12 elements. Reduces number of chunk allocations and realloc overhead for growable collections used in Cranelift/codegen.

## Effect
- Peak free: 85,286 B → 95,540 B (+10 KB).
- Fewer `RawVecInner::finish_grow` events due to larger chunks amortizing growth.

## Outcome
Kept. Larger chunks trade slightly higher per-chunk overhead for fewer allocations. 12 was chosen empirically; later superseded by dynamic chunk sizing.
