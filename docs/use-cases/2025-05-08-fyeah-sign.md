# FYeah Sigh/Button

For a SOAK camp in 2026.

A hanging sign "FYeah!" sign behind a bar with
a separate big red button on a box sitting on the bar.

The sign shows a chill pattern most of the time.

When the button is pressed, the sign does a frantic exciting
pattern.

## Hardware

A sign of 3d-printed letters with led strip in them. ~120 LEDS,
with a microcontroller running lightplayer.

A separate box with a big red button, potentially with its own
lights.

Ideally wireless communication.

## Feature needs

- GPIO input
- Wireless bus sync
- Playlists
- Event-driven switching
